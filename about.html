About SchemeSathi

SchemeSathi is a citizen-focused portal built by Bhavin Garg, a Computer Science engineering student, with the mission to make government schemes accessible, transparent, and easy to understand.

It was born out of a simple realization — millions of citizens miss out on benefits simply because they are unaware of them.

Why I Built SchemeSathi

While exploring government resources, I realized that valuable schemes exist but are difficult to find, understand, and access.

Many people — especially in rural and underserved communities — are unaware of benefits they are eligible for. This gap between opportunity and awareness inspired me to build SchemeSathi.

Helping the Unaware

Navigating multiple government websites can be confusing and discouraging. SchemeSathi solves this by bringing everything together in one place, making it easier for citizens to discover opportunities.

One Portal, All Opportunities

Instead of searching across dozens of portals, citizens can explore healthcare, scholarship, and pension schemes in one unified platform with direct official links.

Technical Growth

SchemeSathi is built using HTML, CSS, TailwindCSS, and modular JSON datasets.

This project helped me grow as a developer by focusing on scalability, responsive design, and real-world user experience.

Challenges Faced

Collecting accurate scheme data and structuring it in a scalable format was one of the biggest challenges. This experience strengthened my problem-solving and system design skills.
