Student Beneficial Schemes

Explore scholarships, loans, and support programs designed to empower students across India.

National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

Overview: Centralized platform for scholarships.

Eligibility: Class 1 to PhD students.

Benefits: Tuition + living expenses.

Official Website Quick Apply

PM Uchchatar Shiksha Protsahan (PM-USP)

Overview: Scholarship for college students.

Eligibility: Meritorious + low-income families.

Benefits: ₹10k–₹20k/year.

Official Website

Post-Matric Scholarship (SC/ST)

Overview: Financial support for higher education.

Eligibility: Income below ₹2.5 lakh.

Benefits: Fees + allowance.

NMMS Scholarship

Overview: Support for Class 9–12 students.

Eligibility: Income below ₹1.5 lakh.

Benefits: ₹12,000/year.

AICTE Pragati & Saksham

Pragati: For girl students.

Saksham: For specially-abled students.

Benefits: ₹50,000/year.

Vidya Lakshmi Portal

Overview: Education loan portal.

Eligibility: Higher education students.

Benefits: Loans up to ₹15 lakh.

Official Website

Skill India / PMKVY

Overview: Skill training program.

Eligibility: Age 18–35.

Benefits: Free training + jobs.
