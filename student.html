Student Beneficial Schemes

Explore scholarships, loans, and support programs designed to empower students across India.

National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

Overview: Centralized platform for scholarships from various ministries.

Eligibility: Students from Class 1 to PhD.

Benefits: Tuition, living expenses, exam fees.

Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Uchchatar Shiksha Protsahan (PM-USP)

Overview: Central Sector Scholarship for college/university students.

Eligibility: Meritorious students from low-income families.

Benefits: ₹10,000 per year for UG, ₹20,000 per year for PG.

Official Website

Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students

Overview: Financial support for SC/ST students pursuing higher education.

Eligibility: Family income below ₹2.5 lakh/year.

Benefits: Tuition fees, maintenance allowance, exam fees.

National Means-Cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS)

Overview: Helps meritorious students from weaker sections continue secondary education.

Eligibility: Class 9–12 students, parental income below ₹1.5 lakh/year.

Benefits: ₹12,000 per year till Class 12.

AICTE Pragati & Saksham Scholarships

Pragati: For girl students in technical education.

Saksham: For differently-abled students in technical courses.

Benefits: ₹50,000 per year for tuition, books, living expenses.

Vidya Lakshmi Education Loan Portal

Overview: Single-window platform for education loans.

Eligibility: Students seeking higher education in India or abroad.

Benefits: Loans up to ₹7.5 lakh (India) and ₹15 lakh (abroad).

Official Website

Skill India & PMKVY

Overview: Vocational training and skill development for youth.

Eligibility: Students aged 18–35.

Benefits: Free training, certification, job placement support.
