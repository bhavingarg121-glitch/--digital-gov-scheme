Student Support Schemes

Scholarships, skill development, and education support programs for students across India.

Featured Student Schemes

National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

Overview: Centralized portal for scholarships offered by the Government of India.

Eligibility: Students from SC/ST/OBC/Minority and economically weaker sections.

Benefits: Direct scholarship transfer to bank accounts.

Official Website

PM Scholarship Scheme (PMSS)

Overview: Scholarships for wards of ex-servicemen and widows.

Eligibility: Students pursuing professional courses.

Benefits: Financial support for tuition and living expenses.

Official Website

AICTE Pragati & Saksham Scholarships

Overview: Scholarships for girl students (Pragati) and differently-abled students (Saksham).

Eligibility: Students admitted to AICTE-approved institutions.

Benefits: Annual financial support for tuition and books.

Official Website

INSPIRE Scholarship

Overview: Scholarship for students pursuing natural and basic sciences.

Eligibility: Top 1% in Class XII or JEE/NEET rank holders.

Benefits: Annual scholarship for higher education in science.

Official Website

National Means-Cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS)

Overview: Scholarships for meritorious students from economically weaker sections.

Eligibility: Class VIII students with family income below ₹1.5 lakh.

Benefits: Annual scholarship to support secondary education.

Official Website
