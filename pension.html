Pension Schemes

Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

Overview: Monthly pension for unorganized workers.

Eligibility: Citizens aged 18–40 years with savings account.

Benefits: Pension between ₹1,000–₹5,000 depending on contributions.

Official Website

Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS)

Overview: Financial assistance to elderly citizens below poverty line.

Eligibility: Citizens aged 60+ from BPL families.

Benefits: Monthly pension credited directly to bank accounts.
