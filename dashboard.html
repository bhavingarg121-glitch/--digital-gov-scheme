About SchemeSathi

SchemeSathi is a citizen-focused portal designed to simplify access to government schemes. Our mission is to empower every individual by providing clear, transparent, and multilingual information about scholarships, pensions, healthcare, and more. We collaborate with NGOs and government bodies to ensure accuracy, trust, and inclusivity.

With responsive design, AI-driven eligibility checks, and a user-friendly interface, SchemeSathi ensures that no citizen is left behind. Together, we build a bridge between people and opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a scheme?

You can apply by clicking on the "Apply for Schemes" button in the Quick Links section. Fill out the required details and submit your application online.

What documents are required?

Required documents vary by scheme, but typically include proof of identity, proof of residence, and income certificates. Check the scheme details for specifics.

How can I track my application?

Use the "Track Application" option in Quick Links. Enter your application ID to view the current status and updates.

Is multilingual support available?

Yes, SchemeSathi supports multiple languages to ensure accessibility for all citizens. You can switch languages from the settings menu.
