Featured Schemes

PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

Overview: ₹6,000 annual income support for small/marginal farmers.

Eligibility: Farmers owning cultivable land.

Benefits: Direct benefit transfer in three installments annually.

Official Website

Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

Overview: ₹5 lakh health insurance coverage per family.

Eligibility: Families identified under SECC database.

Benefits: Free treatment in empaneled hospitals.

Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Overview: Affordable housing for urban and rural families.

Eligibility: Middle-income groups (MIG I & II).

Benefits: Subsidized home loans, interest rate reduction.

Official Website

Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

Overview: Monthly pension for unorganized workers.

Eligibility: Citizens aged 18–40 years with savings account.

Benefits: Pension between ₹1,000–₹5,000 depending on contributions.

Official Website

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

Overview: National campaign to promote education and welfare of girl children.

Eligibility: Families with girl children.

Benefits: Awareness programs, scholarships, and community support initiatives.

Official Website
