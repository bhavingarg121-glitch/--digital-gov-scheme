Farmer Schemes

PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

Overview: ₹6,000 annual income support for small/marginal farmers.

Eligibility: Farmers owning cultivable land.

Benefits: Direct benefit transfer in three installments annually.

Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Overview: Crop insurance against natural calamities.

Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops.

Benefits: Low premium rates, coverage for crop loss.
