Middle Class Support Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Overview: Affordable housing for urban and rural families.

Eligibility: Middle-income groups (MIG I & II).

Benefits: Subsidized home loans, interest rate reduction.

Official Website

Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

Overview: ₹5 lakh health insurance coverage per family.

Eligibility: Families identified under SECC database.

Benefits: Free treatment in empaneled hospitals.
