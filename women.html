Women Empowerment Schemes

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

Overview: Promotes education and welfare of girl children.

Eligibility: All families with girl children.

Benefits: Awareness programs, scholarships, community support.

Stand-Up India Scheme

Overview: Loans for women entrepreneurs.
